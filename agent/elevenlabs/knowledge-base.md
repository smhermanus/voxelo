# ABOUT THE BUSINESS

Joe's Plumbing is a family-run plumbing business based in Cape Town, South Africa. Founded in 2010 by Joe, the business has served thousands of homes and businesses across the greater Cape Town area. We are known for reliable, honest service and transparent pricing.

# SERVICES

We handle all residential and commercial plumbing needs including:

- Burst pipe repairs and emergency pipe work
- Geyser repair and full geyser replacement (all brands)
- Blocked drain clearing (including high-pressure water jetting)
- Leak detection (including hidden leaks behind walls and under floors)
- Bathroom installations (toilets, basins, showers, baths)
- Kitchen plumbing (sinks, dishwasher connections)
- General maintenance and inspections
- Emergency callouts — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for emergencies

We do NOT currently offer: borehole drilling, pool plumbing, industrial/large-scale commercial projects, gas fitting.

# PRICING

Standard callout fee: R450 — this covers the callout and the first 30 minutes of labour on-site.
After the first 30 minutes, additional labour is charged at R150 per 30-minute block.

Emergency callout fee (after hours — outside Mon–Fri 08:00–17:00 and Sat 09:00–13:00): R750.
This covers arrival and the first 30 minutes on-site.

Parts and materials are charged separately at cost plus a 15% handling fee.

Geyser replacement (labour only, excluding geyser unit): from R800 depending on access difficulty.
A standard 150L geyser unit costs approximately R3,500–R5,000 depending on brand.

We provide a written quote before starting any work that exceeds R1,000 in total.
We accept EFT, cash, and major credit cards.

# COMMON QUESTIONS

**How quickly can you come out?**
For standard bookings, we aim to schedule within 1–2 business days. For urgent (non-emergency) issues, we do our best to come same-day or next-day depending on availability. For true emergencies (burst pipes, flooding), we aim to have someone on-site within 60–90 minutes.

**Do I need to be home?**
Yes, an adult (18+) must be present at the property for the plumber to begin work and for you to authorise the quote.

**Do you provide a guarantee on work?**
Yes. All labour is guaranteed for 90 days. Manufacturer warranties apply to all parts and units supplied.

**Can you replace my geyser the same day?**
If the failure is confirmed over the phone and we have a suitable replacement unit in stock, same-day replacement is often possible. Call to confirm availability.

**What should I do if I have a burst pipe right now?**
Turn off your main water supply valve immediately — it is usually near the water meter or outside the property boundary. Then call us. Our emergency team will talk you through next steps while we dispatch.

**Do you work on solar geysers?**
Yes, we service and repair most solar geyser systems, including the backup electric element.

**Do you give free quotes?**
We provide on-site quotes before starting work over R1,000. The standard callout fee applies for the visit; if you proceed with the work, the callout fee is deducted from the final invoice.

**Can I speak to Joe directly?**
Joe manages the team in the field and is not always reachable by phone. The best way to reach him is to leave a message and he or a senior team member will call you back, usually within a few hours during business hours.
