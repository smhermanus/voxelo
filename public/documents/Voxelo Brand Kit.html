01 Logo Lockups & variants

The Voice Wave

A speech bubble cradling a live voice waveform. It draws on the name's root — vox, Latin for voice — and on what Voxelo does: listening and replying across chat, voice, and email. The bars animate when an agent is thinking. The wordmark is set in Sora with a tightened track; the accent dot signals "live."

02 Construction Clear space, sizing & misuse

Give it room

Protect the mark with clear space equal to half the bubble's height on every side. Keep the five waveform bars symmetric and evenly spaced — never crowd, recolor outside the palette, or distort the geometry.

03 Explorations Alternate mark directions

Routes we considered

The Voice Wave won for its direct tie to the name's root, vox, and because it animates so naturally. These directions were strong contenders — kept on file for sub-brands, motion, and future product marks.

Route A

The Vox Bubble

A speech bubble with the brand's V cut into it. Cleanest monogram tie-in — held as the alternate icon.

Route B

Voxel cube

An isometric building block — modular AI for any industry. Distinct, but abstract: it asks you to decode the name first.

Route C

Dialogue

Two interlocking bubbles — customer and agent in sync. Warm and human, but a touch less ownable.
